About Nisarga Spices

Nisarga Spices is a small‑batch brand from Karnataka focused on honest ingredients and home‑style recipes. We bring forest honey, classic masala powders and Malenadu pickles directly from local kitchens to your table.

Every batch is prepared in limited quantities without artificial colours, flavours or preservatives. Our aim is to keep the warmth of homemade food while offering clean, modern packaging you can trust for your family.

How It's Made

Every jar and packet from Nisarga Spices goes through a simple, careful routine so it feels as close to homemade as possible.

Select

We choose seasonal forest honey and spices from trusted growers in and around Karnataka.

Clean & Roast

Spices are cleaned, sun‑dried and gently roasted to lock in natural aroma and flavour.

Grind & Mix

Small batches are stone‑ground or hand‑mixed to keep the texture, colour and taste consistent.

Pack & Seal

Finally, everything is packed in food‑grade containers, labelled clearly and sealed fresh for your kitchen.
