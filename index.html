Nisarga Spices

Nisarga Spices is a small‑batch brand from Karnataka focused on honest ingredients and time‑tested recipes. Every jar is crafted in limited quantities so you get consistent taste and freshness.

From forest‑harvested honey to slow‑roasted spice powders and tangy Amtekayi pickles, our products are free from artificial colours, flavours and preservatives – just real home‑style flavour.

Best‑Selling Nisarga Products

Organic Forest Honey

Raw forest honey collected from the hills of Karnataka. Naturally thick, aromatic and packed with everyday wellness benefits – perfect for morning warm water, desserts or tea.

Amtekayi (Hog Plum) Pickle

Traditional Malenadu‑style hog plum pickle with a bold, tangy and spicy profile. Slow cured in cold‑pressed oil with freshly roasted spices – a perfect side for rice, roti or curd rice.
